# Ninad Bhave

## Aerospace Engineering (Honours) Graduate

Welcome to my professional engineering portfolio.

This repository showcases selected aerospace engineering projects completed throughout my Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) at RMIT University, Australia. The portfolio demonstrates my experience across flight dynamics, autonomous systems, aircraft structures, systems engineering, finite element analysis, CAD design and multidisciplinary aerospace engineering.

---

## Engineering Projects

### Flight Control System Design Optimisation (Capstone)
High Distinction project investigating aircraft flight dynamics, control system optimisation and genetic algorithms using MATLAB/Simulink.

### Autonomous UAV Development
Integration of Autonomous Systems Engineering and Foundations of Artificial Intelligence to develop and validate a conceptual autonomous UAV for intelligent mission planning.

### Pilatus PC-9 Composite Wing Redesign
Composite wing redesign incorporating finite element analysis, structural optimisation and aircraft maintenance principles.

### MiG-21 Aerospace Systems Design
Systems engineering evaluation and modernisation study of the MiG-21 aircraft.

### Aerospace Bracket Design (DFMA)
Design and optimisation of a lightweight aerospace bracket capable of sustaining approximately three times the required design load.

### Aircraft CAD Portfolio
Professional aircraft modelling and component design using CATIA V6 and SolidWorks.
